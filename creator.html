Version 0.6.0-beta

1/29/2026 Almost a year after the last update, this version brings two more blocks: the pulser and the counter. There were several UI improvements and various bug fixes. The top bar should now be easier to access. The toolbar was also added, allowing quick access to up to 10 tools for convenience. Finally, users can now find the app as an executable.
